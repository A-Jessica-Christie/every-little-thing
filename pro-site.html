* Services iProvide *

Website Development is an art form of its own.

I develop optimized and responsive websites accessible on every device, based on the needs of every client, to each their own.

I provide fast websites with best performance based on the performance test result on GTmetrix [A grade guaranteed] and PageSpeed Insight [90-100 score guaranteed].

Website traffic can be tracked from the moment the website has gone live, with an implemented Google Analytics.
I will share the website repository when the project is done, so clients can also save and have the files.

― iCare ―

Website maintenance is a must.

I care about all my clients. Assistance and support will always be provided for free.

I use the Content Management System (CMS) by CloudCannon, which is very user-friendly and fully customizable. Clients can access it to update their website content anywhere, anytime ― even with mobile devices.

To make sure that the website is continuously running smoothly and working properly, I provide ongoing monthly service [Website Care Plan] with a really affordable price for my clients. Security measures and backup is also provided to ensure the safety of the website.

It's not just a design.

When designing a website, a good user interaction (UI) and user experience (UX) is always needed.

I design website not only based on client request, but also a design that will give every user the best experience while surfing the website.

Clients are always welcomed to share their ideas for more unique and personalized designs.
